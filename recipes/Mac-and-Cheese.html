Mac and Cheese

Description

This creamy mac and cheese is made with tender pasta coated in a rich, cheesy sauce. Its the ultimate comfort food, perfect for quick weeknight dinners or as a side dish for special occasions.

Made with cheddar cheese so each bite is smooth, gooey and bursting with flavor

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup (such as Campbells)
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 ½ cups macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain.
  2. Mix together condensed soup and cheese. Using the empty soup can, measure 1 can of milk; mix into the soup mixture. Stir in cooked pasta. Dump into a greased casserole dish, and cover.
  3. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
